Headings are defined by "h1-6"(in angle brackets) h1 represents the most important heading, while h6 represents the least important heading. THe 4 decenting lines with "heading" in it are all heading lines.

Headings can be larger than default sizes too.

Headings can be enlarged by using a style attribute (style="font-size:##px;")
